# Assumptions And Limitations

## Assumptions

- Source datasets are prepared before dashboard use.
- Processed files are small enough for interactive Streamlit display.
- Case-study inputs are stored locally and are not committed to git.
- Screening categories are limited to:
  - Review candidate
  - Current category supported
  - Insufficient evidence

## Limitations

- The starter app does not perform heavy GIS processing.
- Placeholder modules are intentionally simple for a 3-week prototype.
- Results depend on the quality, currency, and coverage of source datasets.
- Missing data should be shown clearly in the dashboard instead of hidden.
